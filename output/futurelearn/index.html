FutureLearn

An online learning platform offering courses and resources for teacher development.

Description

FutureLearn is a digital education platform providing access to a wide array of online courses, specifically designed to enhance the skills and knowledge of educators. It collaborates with universities, colleges, and organizations worldwide to offer high-quality, accessible learning experiences. Teachers can engage in professional development courses that focus on various aspects of education, including teaching methodologies, curriculum design, and classroom management. The platform emphasizes collaborative learning, allowing educators to interact, share insights, and learn from one another. FutureLearn's courses are often free to audit, with options for certifications available for a fee, making it a flexible resource for teachers seeking to improve their practice and stay current with educational trends. With a focus on lifelong learning, FutureLearn supports teachers at all stages of their careers, from new educators to experienced professionals looking to expand their expertise.

Examples

  • Online course on 'Teaching English Online' by the British Council.
  • Professional development course on 'Assessment and Feedback' by the University of Leeds.

Additional Information

  • Offers both free and paid courses, with certificates available for completion.
  • Courses cover a wide range of topics relevant to contemporary education.
