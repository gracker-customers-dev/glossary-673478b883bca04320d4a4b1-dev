Demonstrate Your Mastery Of Each Standard With This Tic Tac Toe Choice Board

A creative and flexible learning tool that allows students to choose how they will demonstrate their understanding of specific educational standards.

Description

The 'Tic Tac Toe Choice Board' is an instructional strategy that provides students with a grid of options, typically arranged in a three-by-three format, where each cell contains a different activity or task aimed at demonstrating mastery of a particular standard. This approach not only promotes student autonomy and engagement but also caters to diverse learning styles by offering varied means of assessment. For example, students might choose to create a presentation, write an essay, develop a project, or engage in a collaborative discussion, depending on their interests and strengths. The design encourages students to think critically about their learning and select tasks that resonate with them, making the assessment process more personalized and meaningful. Teachers can use this tool to ensure that all students have the opportunity to showcase their understanding in a way that is most effective for them, while also aligning with curriculum goals.

Examples

  • Students choose to create a poster that illustrates a scientific concept.
  • Students opt to write a poem that reflects their understanding of a historical event.

Additional Information

  • Ideal for differentiated instruction and student-centered learning.
  • Can be adapted to various subjects and grade levels.
