Class Central

A comprehensive platform for discovering online courses and resources for teacher development.

Description

Class Central is an online aggregator of massive open online courses (MOOCs) and other educational resources, specifically designed to help educators and teachers find professional development opportunities. It allows users to search and explore a wide array of courses offered by various institutions, covering diverse topics relevant to teaching and education. Teachers can enhance their skills, learn new methodologies, and stay updated on educational trends through these courses, which often include certificates upon completion. The platform is user-friendly and offers filters to narrow down searches based on subject, provider, and level of expertise. Additionally, Class Central provides access to user reviews and ratings, enabling educators to make informed decisions about which courses best meet their personal and professional development needs. By consolidating resources from numerous providers, Class Central serves as a one-stop-shop for teachers looking to improve their teaching practices and advance their careers.

Examples

  • Courses on classroom management strategies.
  • Workshops on integrating technology in the classroom.

Additional Information

  • Free and paid courses available.
  • Courses are offered by reputable institutions like Harvard, Stanford, and Coursera.
