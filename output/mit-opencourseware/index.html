MIT OpenCourseWare

A free and open educational resource that provides access to course materials from a wide range of MIT courses.

Description

MIT OpenCourseWare (OCW) is an initiative by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) to publish virtually all MIT course content online for free. Launched in 2002, OCW is designed to enhance teaching and learning worldwide by providing educators, students, and self-learners access to high-quality educational resources. The platform includes a diverse array of materials such as lecture notes, assignments, exams, and video lectures across various disciplines, including science, engineering, humanities, and social sciences. OCW allows educators to incorporate MIT's rigorous academic standards and innovative teaching methods into their own curriculum. By making these materials publicly available, MIT aims to foster a culture of sharing and collaboration in education, empowering individuals everywhere to learn at their own pace and pursue their academic interests without financial barriers. As such, OCW serves as a valuable resource for teacher development, offering inspiration and practical content that can be adapted and utilized in diverse educational settings.

Additional Information

  • OCW has over 2,400 courses available in various subjects.
  • Materials can be accessed without registration or fees.
