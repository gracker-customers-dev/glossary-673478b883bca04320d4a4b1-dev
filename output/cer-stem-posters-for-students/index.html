CER STEM Posters for Students

Visual aids that support students in understanding and applying the Claim, Evidence, Reasoning (CER) framework in STEM education.

Description

CER STEM Posters for Students are educational resources designed to help students articulate their scientific understanding through the structured framework of Claim, Evidence, and Reasoning. These posters serve as visual guides that outline the components of the CER model, making it easier for students to formulate their arguments and support their scientific claims. The posters typically include definitions, examples, and prompts that encourage critical thinking and effective communication of scientific ideas. By using these posters, students can enhance their ability to analyze data, draw conclusions, and present their findings in a clear and organized manner. They are particularly useful in classrooms that emphasize inquiry-based learning and scientific literacy, fostering an environment where students can engage deeply with STEM concepts. Additionally, these resources can be integrated into various activities, such as experiments, research projects, and presentations, making them versatile tools for teachers aiming to improve student outcomes in science education.

Examples

  • A poster illustrating how to construct a CER statement based on a classroom experiment.
  • A visual guide showing examples of strong versus weak claims in a scientific context.

Additional Information

  • Can be used across different grade levels and STEM subjects.
  • Encourages collaboration and discussion among students as they work on their CER tasks.
